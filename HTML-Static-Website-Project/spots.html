My Favorite NYC Spots

I enjoy exploring New York City, and here are some of my favorite spots:

  • Junction Deli (SkyBlue) - Best rolls/heroes/sandwiches ever (in my opinion)
  • Silver Spoon Diner - Has great pizza. A little expensive though.
  • Metropolitan Musuem of Art - Amazing art collections

However, NYC can get crowded and noisy sometimes, which can be challenging.
